Ingredients
- 2 c. bbq sauce
- 2 tbsp. honey
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 tbsp. Hot sauce
- 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts and drumsticks
- kosher salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
- extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
1. Make Sauce: In a large bowl, whisk together BBQ sauce, honey, lime juice, and hot sauce. Reserve 1/2 cup for serving.

2. In a large bowl, toss chicken with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

3. When ready to grill, heat grill to high. Oil grates and add chicken. Baste with marinade and grill 	until charred, 8 minutes per side for breasts, and 10 to 12 minutes per side for legs.
